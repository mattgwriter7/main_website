Image Asides

The have an image up top, and some caption text underneath. These are the default asides, and they don't need a class.

Quote Asides

These have a quote up top, and underneath have the author of the quote. These need two things:

  1. class="quote" in the opening aside tag
  2. a <blockquote> replaces the <img> tag up top
