Bonus Section

Have you ever heard of a "gamebook"? It is a choose-your-own-adventure thingamajig which is half book and half game. They were very popular back in the 1980s for a bit, when Dungeons & Dragons was all the rage.

May I present...

"The Dark Castle of Murwyche"

You can play it right here by clicking the blue "begin" button. It actually works!

My goal is to have this be a Mobile App that serves as a wrapper for an unlimited number of stories. Ideally a community would develop that can write these stories, but for now there is just... me.

The "gamebook" concept combines my two main interests: Writing and Coding.

I am still fleshing out "The Dark Castle...", but I have done the first several passages. I hope you try it out and come to enjoy it. I welcome feeback on the story as well as the app.
Note: Keep in mind they are both very much "works in progress."
