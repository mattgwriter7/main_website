As you can see the H3 does not do that much. Pretty well it is just bolded and serif-ed.

But the H1 and H2s are kinda fancy. They have some CSS-stylin' to make them POP!

There is never top margins or paddings on Heading tags by default. But! They will have bottom-margins. To get rid of a bottom-margin use a class="short" which will strip it completely.
NOTE: The amount of bottom-margin is different for H1, H2, and H3.

Styling Fonts

You can bold, italicize, and underline stuff using normal HTML. (That is: <i>, <b>, and <u>.)

But there are also some shortcuts for "special effects," like this:

  • <b class="o"> = Oswald
  • <b class="r"> = Roboto
  • <b class="m"> = Monospaced

NOTE: A <b> on its own will bold, but when you add one of the shortcut classes above, it is not bolded. (Go figure!)
